Craigslist, a long-awaited redesign




Craigslist has been around since the dark ages...


Link to the website

However, Craigslist has not gone through many updates and as a result, the interface is not very user-friendly.






Accessibility Test Findings

  • Errors: 2
  • Contrast Errors: 275
  • Alerts: 57

The contrast errors were about the hyperlinks and the background having low contrast with each other. The alerts ranged from redundancy to suspicious link text to very small text and many more types. The errors were that there was a missing language option and a missing form label.




There are various other problems with the interface.


No pictures
All hyperlinks
No color
Too much text
Non-existent capitalization
Unnecessary information




And so, I decided to give it a little redesign...
